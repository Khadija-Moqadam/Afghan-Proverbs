Afghan proverbs are short and powerful expressions of everyday life and wisdom. Passed from generation to generation, they reflect the values, humor, and experiences of Afghan culture.

Featured Proverbs

صبر تلخ است، اما میوه‌اش شیرین است

Patience is bitter, but its fruit is sweet.

This proverb encourages patience during hard times because good outcomes take time.

با یک دست دو تربوز برداشته نمی‌شود

You can't carry two melons in one hand.

You can't do two difficult tasks at the same time. Focus on one thing.
