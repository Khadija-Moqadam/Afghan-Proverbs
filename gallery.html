کوه به کوه نمی‌رسد، آدم به آدم می‌رسد

Mountains don’t meet, but people do.

People meet again, even unexpectedly.

اگر نخواهی رسوا شوی، همرنگ جماعت شو

If you don’t want shame, blend with the crowd.

Sometimes it's wise to go with the flow.

تا مرد سخن نگفته باشد، عیب و هنرش نهفته باشد

Until a man speaks, his flaws and virtues stay hidden.

Speech reveals a person's true self.

هر که بامش بیش، برفش بیشتر

The taller the roof, the more snow it catches.

With more comes more responsibility.
