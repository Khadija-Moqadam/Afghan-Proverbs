Detailed Proverb

صبر تلخ است، اما میوه‌اش شیرین است

Patience is bitter, but its fruit is sweet.

This proverb teaches us that although being patient can be very difficult, it often leads to good results. It's a message of hope and strength, especially in difficult times. In Afghan culture, patience is a deeply respected virtue, often linked to wisdom and strength of character.
